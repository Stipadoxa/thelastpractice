Discover the Music

Reclaim The Music

You are the newest member of a band on the verge of breaking up. In a world beset with algorithmic careers and generative push-button-creativity in the name of "AI", what is the role of art, and inspiration any more?

It's up to you to help remind the band of their inspirations, by sifting through the records of foundational artists, and craft an entirely new song.

The Experience

Full VR Immersion

Experience our exclusive AVP (Apple Vision Pro) in cutting-edge virtual reality that transports you into the heart of the music scene.

32 Influential Records

Dive into a curated collection of 32 legendary albums that shaped modern music. Explore and draw inspiration from these foundational works to reignite the band's creative spark.

Create Something New

Use the inspiration from musical legends to help your bandmates craft an entirely original song that rediscovers their artistic purpose.
